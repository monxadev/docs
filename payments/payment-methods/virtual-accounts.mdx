title: "Virtual Accounts"
---

Virtual Accounts allow customers to pay by transferring funds into a **unique bank account number** assigned by Monxa. The VA can be **dynamic** (single-use) or **static** (reusable). When the customer makes a transfer, the provider notifies Monxa, and Monxa updates the merchant via webhook.

## Payment Flow

```mermaid
sequenceDiagram
    participant Customer
    participant Merchant
    participant Monxa
    participant QRProvider as VA Provider (Bank)
    autonumber
    Customer ->> Merchant: Select Virtual Account
    Merchant ->> Monxa: Create VA charge (amount, expiry)
    Monxa ->> QRProvider: Request VA issuance
    QRProvider ->> Monxa: Return VA details (account no, bank, expiry)
    Monxa ->> Merchant: Return VA info + instructions
    Merchant ->> Customer: Show VA account number & payment instructions

    Customer ->> QRProvider: Transfers funds via ATM / mobile banking
    QRProvider ->> Monxa: Notify credit received
    Monxa ->> Merchant: Webhook: charge.succeeded
    Monxa ->> Merchant: Settle funds into balance
    Merchant ->> Customer: Show success confirmation
```
